How to Use This Generator

Basic Controls

Library Buttons: At the top, toggle to save/load your favorite prompts
Prompt Area: Type your image description here
Style Buttons: Click any style button to generate images
Dimension Options: Square (768768), Portrait (512768), Landscape (768512)
Amount Setting: Control how many images are generated at once
SFW Button: Activates filters to reduce (but not eliminate) adult content
Refine Images: Click the "M" button on any generated image to fine-tune it

Advanced Features

Customize Styles: Create your own style buttons with custom colors and prompts
Rainbow Toggle: Click any rainbow text to switch between rainbow and white text
Mobile Friendly: Swipe left/right to see more style buttons on phone
Extremetize: Super-focuses one word in your prompt for stronger emphasis
Personize: Adds race, hair color, and eye color details to your prompt
Placeize: Enhances setting details in your generated images

Community

Join our monthly image contest! Look for the link in the bottom menu.

Note

Even with SFW mode activated, some prompts may still result in adult content.
